## Phase 1 (Done)

- Core rules (`max-file-lines`, `max-function-lines`, `no-orphan-todos`, `no-ai-obvious-comments`)
- ESLint v8/v9 support
- CLI `init` for Vite, Next.js, and Elysia.

## Phase 2 (In Progress)

- CLI Optimization for NestJS, Express, and SvelteKit.

## Phase 3 (Future)

- AI Hallucination Guard
- Automated CI Bot
